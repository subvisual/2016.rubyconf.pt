Auditório Vita


The venue for RubyConf PT will be Auditório Vita, a beautiful piece of architecture. Only 5 minutes away from downtown, this is the perfect place to welcome you.

Where to stay?

To get access to these prices, you need to send an email to the hotel. Mention that you're attending RubyConf, the room type and your check-in and check-out dates. These special conditions apply for your whole stay, not just the days of the conference, so you can take the chance to spend some time in the country.
